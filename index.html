About Me

I am an 18-year-old sophomore at The University of Texas at Dallas majoring in Computer Science. Ever since I was a young girl, I just knew that everything that I wanted to do was within the domain of computing. I have always been fascinated by the way that computers work, and I have always wanted to learn more about them! So one day in middle school, I brought home a Raspberry Pi 3, and it was my first experience with linux. After then, I dabbled with Arch (mostly to just say that I had used Arch), Kali, and Ubuntu, but somehow forgot my love for computers along the way in high school. I became an ambition-less blob that played and excessive amount of Hightower on Team Fortress 2. Then one day, I had to make a decision. I could not decide what I wanted to do in my future that could fulfill my academic urges to keep learning the things that I liked. I then remembered that I did indeed love computers, and I felt like a total dummy. I now use Fedora Workstation, and have been actually learning the command line!
